# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 4, 2026

Community Hub ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications, web applications, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

This policy complies with the **Ethiopian Personal Data Protection Proclamation No. 808/2013** and other applicable Ethiopian laws.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you register for an account, we collect:
- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number (optional)
- Username
- Password (hashed, never stored in plain text)
- Profile photo (optional)

### Payment Information
For manual payment verification, we collect:
- Bank account details (where you sent money)
- Sender name
- Sender phone (optional)
- Transaction reference number (optional)
- Receipt screenshot/PDF
- Date of payment

### Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- IP address
- Device type and OS version
- App version
- Activity timestamps
- Push notification tokens (for delivering notifications)

### Content
- Posts, comments, and reactions you create
- Tickets you create or purchase
- Votes you cast
- Profile information you provide

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for:
- **Account management** — creating and maintaining your account
- **Service delivery** — enabling core features (posts, votes, tickets, payments)
- **Communication** — sending you important notifications and updates
- **Security** — preventing fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
- **Improvement** — analyzing usage patterns to improve the Service
- **Legal compliance** — complying with Ethiopian laws and regulations
- **Customer support** — responding to your inquiries

We **do not** use your information for:
- Advertising or marketing to third parties
- Selling to data brokers
- Profiling for automated decisions with legal effects

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing (Ethiopia)

Under Ethiopian law (Proclamation No. 808/2013), we process your data based on:
- **Consent** — for marketing and non-essential cookies
- **Contract performance** — to provide the Service you signed up for
- **Legal obligation** — to comply with Ethiopian law
- **Legitimate interest** — for security, fraud prevention, and service improvement

## 4. Data Sharing

We **do not sell** your personal data. We share data only with:

- **Service providers** — Cloudinary (image hosting), Firebase (push notifications), email service providers — under strict data processing agreements
- **Legal authorities** — when required by Ethiopian law or valid court order
- **Other users** — limited public information (your name, posts, comments) is visible to other users of the Service

We transfer data **only within Ethiopia** by default. Cross-border transfers require your explicit consent, except where required by law.

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption in transit** — TLS 1.2+ for all data transmission
- **Encryption at rest** — sensitive data encrypted in the database
- **Hashed passwords** — bcrypt with cost factor 12
- **Access controls** — role-based access for staff
- **Audit logs** — sensitive actions are logged
- **Regular backups** — encrypted, stored in secure location
- **Penetration testing** — periodic security audits

Despite our efforts, no system is 100% secure. You use the Service at your own risk.

## 6. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Account data | Until you delete your account + 30 days |
| Posts/comments | Until you delete them |
| Payment proofs | 7 years (Ethiopian tax law requirement) |
| Audit logs | 2 years |
| Backups | 90 days rolling |
| Inactive accounts | 2 years, then anonymized |

After retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

## 7. Your Rights

Under Ethiopian law, you have the right to:
- **Access** — request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction** — update or correct inaccurate data
- **Deletion** — request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Restriction** — limit how we process your data
- **Portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Object** — object to certain types of processing
- **Withdraw consent** — for consent-based processing
- **Complain** — to the Ethiopian Data Protection Authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at **privacy@communityhub.com**.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under **13 years old**. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, contact us immediately.

## 9. International Data Transfers

By default, your data is stored on servers in **Ethiopia**. Some services we use (Firebase, Cloudinary) may transfer data outside Ethiopia for processing. We ensure such transfers comply with Ethiopian data protection law and use providers with adequate data protection guarantees.

## 10. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and tracking:
- **Essential** — authentication tokens (required)
- **Analytics** — anonymized usage statistics (optional)
- **No advertising cookies**

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be notified via:

- Notice on the login screen

Continued use after notification constitutes acceptance.
