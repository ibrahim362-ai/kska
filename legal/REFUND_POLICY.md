# Refund Policy

**Last updated:** June 4, 2026

This Refund Policy applies to all paid transactions on Community Hub.

## 1. Eligible Refunds

### Membership Subscriptions
- **7-day money-back guarantee** for first-time purchases
- Refund prorated based on unused days
- Automatic cancellation at period end (if auto-renew is off)

### Event Tickets
- **Full refund** if cancelled 7+ days before the event
- **50% refund** if cancelled 3-7 days before the event
- **No refund** if cancelled less than 3 days before the event
- **Full refund** if the event is cancelled by the organizer

### Other Paid Services
- Determined case-by-case

## 2. Refund Process

1. Contact **refunds@communityhub.com** with:
   - Your account email
   - Transaction reference number
   - Reason for refund
2. We review and respond within **3 business days**
3. Approved refunds processed within **14 business days** to original payment source

## 3. Non-Refundable Items

- Service fees (if any)
- Bank transaction fees
- Payments already used (e.g., ticket checked in)
- Fraudulent or disputed payments
- Account terminated for Terms violation

## 4. Disputes

If you disagree with a refund decision:
1. Email **legal@communityhub.com**
2. We respond within 7 days
3. Unresolved disputes go to Ethiopian arbitration (Addis Ababa)
